Pastrami

A simple, delicious and affordable way to prepare silverside.

Make lots of this for your sandwiches or to serve with pickles and mustard.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb uncorned silverside beef
  • 1/2 cup coarse salt
  • 2 tbsp black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp ground allspice

Procedure

  1. Mix the salt, peppercorns, coriander seeds, and garlic in a bowl.
  2. Rub the mixture all over the uncorned silverside, making sure it is evenly coated.
  3. Wrap the beef tightly in plastic wrap and place it in a shallow dish.
  4. Refrigerate for 5 to 7 days, turning the beef over every 24 hours.
  5. After 5 to 7 days, rinse the beef under cold water and pat it dry.
  6. Mix the paprika, mustard seeds, and allspice in a bowl.
  7. Place the beef in a roasting pan and brush it with the mixture.
  8. Preheat the oven to 150 °C.
  9. Roast the beef for 2 1/2 to 3 hours, or until it reaches an internal temperature of 75 °C.
  10. Let the pastrami cool completely, then slice it thinly and serve. Enjoy!
